Somali Cat

Somali cats are well known for their incredible paw dexterity, which allows them to hold and manipulate objects as effectively as a monkey.

  • Origin: Somalia
  • Weight Range: 8-12 lbs
  • Hair Length: Long
  • Colors: Red, Ruddy, Blue, Fawn, Sorrel

Energetic, affectionate and intelligent, coupled with its smooth and rich fur, this cat breed is sure to win you over!
The Somali cat has been appropriately nicknamed as the "fox cat" due to its uniquely fluffy tail and large ears, as well as its traditionally vibrant red fur.
